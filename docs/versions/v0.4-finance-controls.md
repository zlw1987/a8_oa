# V0.4 Finance Controls

## Scope

This version added finance policy controls around actual spending and company card usage.

## Delivered Capabilities

- Finance app with over-budget policy configuration.
- Policy matching by request type, department, project type, payment method, currency, amount threshold, percent threshold, and priority.
- Policy actions for allow, warning, review, amendment required, and block.
- Accounting review items for purchase and travel over-budget actuals.
- Request closeout blocking when unresolved accounting review items remain.
- Purchase and travel actual review flows aligned with accounting review status.
- Requester self-review prevention for actual spending review.
- Company card transaction capture.
- Card transaction allocation to purchase or travel requests.
- Card match status updates for unmatched, partially matched, matched, and reviewed states.
- Duplicate card transaction detection support.

## Key Apps

- `finance`
- `purchase`
- `travel`
- `projects`

## Regression Coverage

- Over-budget actual expense policy behavior.
- Accounting review queue behavior.
- Purchase and travel close blocking with pending review items.
- Card transaction allocation and duplicate detection.
- Full purchase/travel lifecycle compatibility after finance controls were added.
